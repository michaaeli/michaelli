FitConnect Application

I developed a comprehensive fitness app that allowed users to create personalized exercise plans and engage with a community by sharing posts and interacting with others. The app's frontend was designed using Dart and Flutter, where I focused on creating a seamless and dynamic user interface that optimized user experience and interaction. The app included a variety of features, such as the ability to customize workout routines, track progress, and interact socially with other fitness enthusiasts through posts and comments. For backend functionality, I integrated Google Firebase to manage user authentication, ensuring secure login and account management. Additionally, I built a robust Firebase database capable of storing data for over 1,000 users, including personal information, workout plans, and social posts, allowing for real-time data synchronization and ensuring scalability as the user base grows. Through this project, I successfully merged fitness tracking with social networking, creating an engaging platform for users to stay motivated and connected on their fitness journeys.
